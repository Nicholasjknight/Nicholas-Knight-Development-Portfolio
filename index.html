About Nicholas Knight

Full-Stack Developer & Automation Specialist

I'm Nicholas Knight, a full-stack developer and entrepreneur who builds solutions that work while you sleep. What started as curiosity about making computers do tedious tasks has evolved into Knight Logics - a practice focused on creating sophisticated automation systems that solve real-world business problems.

My Philosophy: The best code is code that runs itself. Whether it's Python scripts monitoring business processes, API integrations connecting platforms seamlessly, or web applications that adapt in real-time - I approach every project asking "How can this be completely automated?"

From video processing pipelines that automatically capture, compress, and upload content, to business automation systems that handle workflows 24/7, I combine technical expertise with business acumen to create solutions that scale.

Professional Experience

My automation solutions have processed thousands of files, streamlined operations for multiple businesses, and created seamless integrations between platforms like Discord, YouTube, and OpenAI APIs. I bring both the technical skills to build complex systems and the business understanding to ensure they deliver real value.
