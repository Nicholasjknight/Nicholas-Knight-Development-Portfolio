Cookie Policy

Last updated: September 26, 2025

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better user experience and understand how you interact with our site.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled:

  • Session management
  • Security features
  • Basic functionality

Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our website:

  • Page views and user behavior
  • Traffic sources and patterns
  • Performance optimization data

Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your choices and settings:

  • Language preferences
  • Theme selections
  • Form data retention

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies
  • Allow only essential cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for future cookies

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies, such as:

  • Google Analytics (if implemented)
  • Social media plugins
  • Content delivery networks
